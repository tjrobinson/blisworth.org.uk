Triple Cottages


Taken from an upstairs room in the Royal Oak - date uncertain.


Taken by GF before much modernisation.  He found a few 17th century features such as a
tiny sandstone lined window, bricked up in the 19th century.

The trio now possess a pleasant array of tiles, corrugated iron and thatch covering as shown
in the picture below.  The tiled part is a 1970s extension which replaces an out-shed.  The row
is listed.
