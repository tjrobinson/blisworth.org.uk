The Freeston business began in 1910 when E. T. Freeston with his sons arrived at the wheelwright forge, High Street.  The main
work evolved into undertaking and servicing cycles and early motor vehicles incl. farmers tractors etc.  From the experiences of
one son in the Navy in WWII jobs involving battery charging for early radios and simple repairs became an important line.
