The 1727-1729 Grafton Survey Map of Blisworth

It was the convention before about 1800 to arrange a map as a bird's eye view and
    endeavour to show chimneys, windows and roofs as faithfully as possible.

    Explore the village, as reported in pages of this website, with a bird's eye view!
