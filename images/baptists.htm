'Church' or 'Chapel' - notes by John Goodridge made in c. 1830 refers to the building
as a chapel and to the institution or movement, it seems, as a church.  On this website
it is intended to do the same.  There is no intention to offend - I would like to hear from
anyone who can advise on whether, in modern usage, the building should be called a Church.
