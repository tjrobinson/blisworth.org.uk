Blisworth Census Data - Analysed

Authors:   H. Spurrier,  B. Andrews  &  R. Freeston
Reproduced here by courtesy of the Blisworth Heritage Society

The information presented here is based on the UK censuses for Blisworth from 1841 to 1901 and thus covers the Victorian period. It is the product of many hours work by the three authors indicated above. The intermediate source was in MS Powerpoint presentations given by Hilary Spurrier and Beryl Andrews to Friday meetings of the Blisworth Heritage Society. In fact two presentations were given, one in March 2012 and the other in March 2013, these being entitled "Parts I and II - The People of Blisworth in the Victorian Period".
