BLISWORTH POSTERS 1950 - 1985

Apologies for the fact that these posters are not displayed in perfect date order and that some posters have no indication of their year of 'creation'.

It is hoped the posters go a little way to supplementing the rather sparse coverage of the period in the articles section of the website.

Collecting together the posters and scanning them to good quality picture files was the much appreciated efforts of Robin Freeston for the Blisworth Heritage Society.

Not all posters give information on the name of the group or society of origin. See here a list of societies that are mentioned and compare with the wide range we now possess.
