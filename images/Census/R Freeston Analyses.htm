Robin Freeston's Census Analyses, 1841-1911

Robin Freeston has spent many hours with a laptop going through all the presently available census sheets with a view to helping future studies.  He has run a count on all people's ages,  surveyed lace-making and railway employment as well as all other professions.  His work facilitated members of the Blisworth Heritage Society in presenting talks on the people and professions of Blisworth.  The links for downloading MSExcel files are listed below.
