In the table above are listed all the camps for which there is a photographic record. All are black and white photos, taken by George Freeston during the period when he was the Scout Leader or Assistant Scout Leader, with the exception of camps in the Lake District which was relatively recent and recorded in colour.

Below are collected a few of the notable shots - it would be too
confusing to present them all. For each venue George had mounted
4 or 5 pictures on a A4 card and installed them in a binder (since lost).
Names are given if they are known.
